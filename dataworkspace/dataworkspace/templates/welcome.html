Get started on Data Workspace

Find out more about how you can use Data Workspace to discover, access and analyse data.

Watch this short video to find out about the benefits of Data Workspace.

Discover Data

Use our searchable data catalogue to explore the 800+ (and growing) datasets we have available. Get started by viewing some of our open datasets or search the data catalogue below.

Easily access data

You can preview, filter and download data using our enhanced preview feature, available on all our data catalogue pages.

Watch our video about previewing data.

Automate your reporting

Design your own dashboards using our data visualisation tools or browse existing reports and analyses.

Share your analysis to collaborate with colleagues.

Upload and analyse data

Upload your data securely to Data Workspace or use data in the catalogue to do analysis in our tools, such as RStudio, JupyterLab or Theia.

Learn more about our tools.

Learn with Learning Pathways

Learning Pathways is our online training. You can select what is relevant for you and use them as needed.
