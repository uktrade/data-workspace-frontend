Who needs to approve this visualisation

To publish this visualisation, it needs approval from three users:

  • yourself: approve it at the bottom of this page
  • a peer reviewer: make them a member of your Gitlab project and notify them. They will then be able to view the visualization at: https://data.trade.gov.uk/visualisations/
  • the Data Workspace Team: contact us so we can work out timeframes for review.
