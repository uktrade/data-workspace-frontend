Request access to tools

To get access to tools you need to have completed the Security and Data Protection training. This training is mandatory and needs to be completed every 12 months.

Once you've verified your training is up to date, you'll get access to:

  • Quicksight
  • pgAdmin
  • Data Explorer
  • RStudio
  • JupyterLab Python
  • Theia

How to verify your training is up to date

You need to upload a screenshot that shows your training has been:

  • successfully completed
  • completed in the last 12 months
