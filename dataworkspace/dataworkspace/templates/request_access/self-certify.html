Get access to tools

To get access to tools you need to have completed the Security and Data Protection training. It's mandatory for all DBT staff and needs to be completed every 12 months.

Once you have verified your training is up to date, you will get access to:

  • Quicksight
  • pgAdmin
  • Data Explorer
  • RStudio
  • JupyterLab Python
  • Theia
  • Your Files

How to verify your training is up to date

You need to give us the date that's on your Security and Data Protection certificate by entering it into the boxes below.
