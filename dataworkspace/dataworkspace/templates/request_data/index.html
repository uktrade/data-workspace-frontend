Request data

Tell us about data you want added to the Data Workspace catalogue.

How long it takes

It should take you about 5 minutes to complete this form. We will respond to you within 5 working days.

Before you start

You will be asked to provide details on what the data is used for, where it is currently held, and the names of the information asset owner or manager. You will also be asked to provide details on the licensing of the data, and to confirm that such licensing permits the storing of the data on Data Workspace. You should have this information before you start - if you are not sure of any of these details, please make a note in the field provided.
